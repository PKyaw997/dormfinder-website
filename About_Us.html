About Us

Dorm Finder is a company that focuses on providing residential services to college students throughout there time present. Students are able to choose from a variety of quality houses that are all in the area of their designated school. Our goal is to provide a safe and friendly environment for all residents that allows no trouble going in and out of school. We want everyone to feel a safe and comfortable place at every house we provide.

Locations provides houses for the designated college that the student is attending. Weather it is other people listing their houses, we provide a program that allows us to find open houses near the area that are available and meets the standards of the student requests.

Pricing due to different areas of houses that are available around every college, pricing will vary. As others are given the option to list their own house, the quality and size would affect the cost.

Can I not reside with another?

We allow the options to stay with another resident or reside alone at a cost.
