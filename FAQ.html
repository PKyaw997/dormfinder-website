FAQ

Listing FAQ Questions and Answers

(1) Does (our site) screen listings?

In order to create a safe marketplace and ensure the accuracy of listings on the ** network, we verify all listings submitted through the Add a Property feature

(2) How do I report a scam?

But if you come across a suspicious listing, we want to know! There's two ways to bring it to our attention. If you're on the website, flag a suspicious listing by clicking the black flag icon at the bottom of every listing that says Report an Issue. You can also review the listing with a customer service representative by giving us a call at (805) 999-9999.

(3) How to apply to rentals (Need site specific details)

When you submit an application, it's securely shared with the listing's property owner or manager. We will let them know you've applied and they can review your application right away.

(4) What info do I need to apply?(Renter)

You'll enter details about yourself, including:
• Your name, contact info, and preferred move-in date
• Any co-applicants, minors, guarantors, or pets who will be living with you
• Residence history, employment, and income details, and references
• Identification info • Vehicle information

(6) Why was my listing rejected?(Property Owner)

There are many reasons for property to be rejected, please contact customer support for your specific reasons
